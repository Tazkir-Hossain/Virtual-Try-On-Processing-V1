How It Works

1

Object Detection: Gemini AI identifies clothing regions in your photo with zero-shot detection

2

ROI Key Points: System generates precise key points within the detected region of interest

3

Segmentation Mask: SAM-2 creates detailed binary masks for accurate clothing boundaries

4

AI Generation: Imagen 3 generates and inpaints realistic clothing onto your image

💡 Tip: For best results, use clear, well-lit photos with the person facing forward
